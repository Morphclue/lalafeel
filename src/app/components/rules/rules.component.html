Rules

We want to make the experience in our free company fun and inclusive. Everyone should feel welcome.
Keep in mind that you also represent our free company as a member.
Therefore we came up with a set of rules that must be adhered to so that this experience is preserved.

    General

    • Treat others the way that you would want to be treated.
    • Breaking the rules can result in being removed from the free company.
    • The same applies for breaking Square Enix's Terms of Service.

    In-game chat

    • No insulting, harassing or hate speech.
    • No sexism, racism or discrimination of any kind.
    • No spamming.

    Discord

    • Read the rules inside the text-channel #rules in our Discord-Server.
    • Rename your nickname to your FFXIV name.
